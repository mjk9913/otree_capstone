You are: Participant A
You have an endowment of ${{player.endow}} to begin with. With each round, you are capable of earning a $12 in additional reward.
However, whether you are awarded the additional reward is dependent on Participant B's performance of completing a certain task provided.

Your probability of receiving the additional reward increases proportionally to the number of times Participant B successfully accomplishes the task. In other words, you are more likely to receive the additional reward as Participant B successfully finishes the task for a greater number of times.

Once Participant B finishes performing the tasks, you will be informed to whether you have received the additional reward based on the probability adjusted by Participant B's performance.

For Participant B to participate in completing the certain task, you are required to compensate $5 to Participant B from your endowment. You have two ways of compensating Participant B:

1. Constant wage (0):
Participant B will be compensated regardless of whether you receive the additional reward. To select this option, choose the compensation choice as 0.

2. Performance-contigent pay (1-20):
Participant B will only be compensated if you receive the additional reward. To select this option, select the compensation choice as the minimum number of tasks Pariticpant B has to accomplish.
